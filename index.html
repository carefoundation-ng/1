About Us

Founded in 2023, Care Foundation provides rapid-response aid to communities devastated by disaster and conflict. We believe that immediate intervention is more than just relief—it is the first step toward restoring dignity and hope for those who need it most.

Projects

At Care Foundation, our work is guided by a clear mission, a bold vision for the future, and strong core values that shape everything we do to support and protect vulnerable women and children.

Mission

Care Foundation’s mission is champion the protection of children's rights, ensure their basic needs are met, and create opportunities that empower them to achieve their full capabilities.

Vission

Care Foundation envisions a world where women and children, particularly the most vulnerable, are protected, empowered, and provided with equal opportunities to thrive, with their voices respected and their rights fully upheld.

Our values

Care, Respect, Integrity, Trust, Accountability, and Sustainability (CRITAS)

Our Programs

Behind every program we implement is a commitment to transform lives and restore hope. Our work focuses on addressing urgent humanitarian needs while creating sustainable solutions that protect rights, expand opportunities, and strengthen communities for the future.

Recovery

Care Foundation supports communities transitioning from crisis to stability by strengthening livelihoods, rebuilding local systems, and restoring economic opportunities. Through skills development, community empowerment, and sustainable initiatives, we help families move beyond survival toward long-term resilience and self-reliance. Rebuilding Lives and Restoring Hope

Learn More

Protection

We work to safeguard the rights, dignity, and wellbeing of vulnerable populations, particularly women and children affected by conflict, displacement, and poverty. Our protection programs provide safe spaces, psychosocial support, and community-based protection mechanisms that prevent violence, exploitation, and abuse

Learn More

Education

Care Foundation promotes access to safe, inclusive, and quality education for children and young people, especially in crisis-affected communities. By supporting learning centers, teacher capacity, and educational resources, we help ensure that every child has the opportunity to learn, grow, and build a better future.

Learn More

WASH

Access to clean water and proper sanitation is essential for health and dignity. Our WASH programs provide safe water systems, improved sanitation facilities, and hygiene promotion to reduce waterborne diseases and improve the overall wellbeing of communities.

Learn More

Health

We support community health by strengthening access to essential health services, promoting disease prevention, and improving health awareness. Our interventions focus on maternal and child health, primary healthcare access, and community-based health systems that improve long-term wellbeing. Health for All, especially the vulnerable women and children

Learn More

Nutrition

Care Foundation addresses malnutrition by providing life-saving nutrition support to vulnerable children and mothers. Through community screening, nutrition education, and targeted feeding programs, we help prevent and treat malnutrition while promoting healthier communities. Fighting malnutrition today, so children thrive tomorrow.

Learn More

Emergency Response

When crises occur, Care Foundation delivers rapid humanitarian assistance to affected populations. Our emergency response programs provide life-saving support including food, shelter, water, and protection services to communities impacted by conflict, disasters, or displacement. Providing immediate assistance and support in crises.

Learn More

Peace Building

Sustainable development requires peaceful and cohesive communities. Our peacebuilding initiatives promote dialogue, community mediation, and social cohesion, helping to prevent conflict and strengthen trust among diverse groups within communities.
